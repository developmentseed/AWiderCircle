The Neighbor-to-Neighbor Program, A Wider Circle's largest program, helps families moving out of shelters or otherwise living without their basic need items.

The most commonly requested items are beds, dressers, and kitchen tables, truly the basic necessities for the home. By providing these items to families in need, we're helping to create the stability and independence that all families deserve.
